About Us

Who We Are

Dedicated to Excellence in Pharmaceutical Pellets

JSR Pharma is a premier proprietorship firm based in Jeedimetla, Hyderabad, specializing in the manufacturing and supply of high-quality pharmaceutical pellets.

Under the visionary leadership of B. Sandhya Rani (Proprietor), we have rapidly established ourselves as a trusted partner for formulation companies. Our facility is designed to meet strict quality standards, ensuring that every batch of excipients we produce serves as the perfect core for your finished dosage forms.
