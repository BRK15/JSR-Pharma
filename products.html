Our Products

High-quality excipient cores for pharmaceutical formulations

1. Sugar Pellets / Spheres

Standard quality pellets used as a core for drug layering. Ideal for sustained-release or extended-release formulations. Available in various mesh sizes to suit specific requirements.

2. Microcrystalline Cellulose (MCC) Pellets

Highly stable, chemically inert cores suitable for layering sensitive APIs. Our MCC pellets offer excellent friability and uniform sphericity, ensuring consistent coating thickness.

3. Lactose Pellets

Designed for specific solubility profiles. These pellets provide a robust core for active ingredients where lactose compatibility is preferred.

4. Starch Pellets

Cost-effective and biodegradable cores. Manufactured using high-grade starch to ensure purity and functionality in dosage forms.

5. Dummy Pellets (NPS)

Non-Pareil Seeds (NPS) available in multiple grades. Perfect inert carriers for a wide range of pharmaceutical applications.
